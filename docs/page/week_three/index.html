Thursday

What does your data say? How can you write down exactly what is in your dataset, for the benefit of

  1. yourself
  2. future collaborators
  3. total strangers
  4. even computers!

The answer is to use a machine-readable metadata format. Metadata is “data about data”, and in this lecture we will practice writing several types of machine-readable metadata.
